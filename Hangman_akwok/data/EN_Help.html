HangMan

Hangman is a guessing word game. The word to guess is represented by a row of dashes, giving the number of letters, numbers and category. If the guessing player suggests a letter or number which occurs in the word,

the other player(Here is the computer) writes it in all its correct positions. If the suggested letter or number does not occur in the word, the other player draws one element of the hanged man stick figure as a tally mark. The game is over when:

1.The guessing player completes the word, or guesses the whole word correctly
